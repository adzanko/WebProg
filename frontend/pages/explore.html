Discover Types

1. The Leader

Characteristics: Assertive, confident, and decisive. Tends to take charge in situations, enjoys challenges, and is highly motivated.

Traits: High in Extraversion and Conscientiousness.

2. The Creative

Characteristics: Imaginative, curious, and open to new ideas. Enjoys exploring new possibilities, thinking outside the box, and expressing themselves artistically.

Traits: High in Openness and moderate in Extraversion.

3. The Caregiver

Characteristics: Empathetic, nurturing, and kind. Enjoys helping others, values harmony, and strives to make people feel safe and supported.

Traits: High in Agreeableness and low in Neuroticism.

4. The Analyzer

Characteristics: Logical, detail-oriented, and introspective. Likes to solve problems, think critically, and understand how things work.

Traits: High in Conscientiousness and moderate in Openness.

5. The Adventurer

Characteristics: Energetic, spontaneous, and thrill-seeking. Loves new experiences, enjoys taking risks, and thrives on excitement.

Traits: High in Extraversion and Openness.
